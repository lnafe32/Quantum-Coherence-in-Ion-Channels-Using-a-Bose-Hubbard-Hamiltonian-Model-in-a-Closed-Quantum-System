Bose-Hubbard Quantum Phase Transition

Current State: Mott Insulator (Particles localized)
How to use: Adjust the slider to change the ratio of Repulsion (U) to Hopping (J).
  • High U/J (Right): Repulsion dominates. Particles are scared to overlap and lock into exactly one per well (Mott Insulator).
  • Low U/J (Left): Hopping dominates. Particles spread out into wide, overlapping quantum waves (Superfluid).
